---
title: "Entities"
---

Entities are distinct objects, concepts, or elements that can be identified and tracked across your content. Mixpeek provides powerful entity recognition and management capabilities.

## Entity Types

### Text Entities

- **Named Entities**: People, organizations, locations
- **Keywords**: Important terms and phrases
- **Concepts**: Abstract ideas and themes
- **Custom Entities**: User-defined categories

### Visual Entities

- **Objects**: Physical objects in images/videos
- **Faces**: Detected and recognized faces
- **Scenes**: Environmental contexts
- **Actions**: Detected activities

### Custom Entities

- **Domain-Specific**: Industry-specific entities
- **Brand Elements**: Logos, products, assets
- **User-Defined**: Custom entity categories
- **Composite Entities**: Combined entity types
