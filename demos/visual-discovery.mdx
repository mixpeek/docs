Visual discovery is the process of finding visually similar items to a given image or video. This is useful for a variety of use cases, such as:

- **Creative Inspiration and Mood Boards**: Find inspiration by discovering similar styles or themes
- **Product Design and Development**: Find similar products to better understand consumer preferences
- **Real Estate and Interior Design**: Showcase properties or styles to help clients find similar options
- **Fashion and Apparel**: Provide personalized fashion recommendations
- **Travel and Tourism**: Showcase destinations, helping users find similar places
- **Event Planning**: Organize themes and setups, allowing clients to find event inspirations
- **Education and Research**: Provide visual resources for exploring related topics
- **Health and Wellness**: Brands can share workouts and recipes, helping users find similar content
- **Content Creation and Management**: Creators can organize visual assets and streamline content production
- **Personal Use and Social Sharing**: Users can create and share boards based on their interests, connecting with others
